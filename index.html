Explore PRAJNA

After three successful years, PRAJNA returns grandly on April 6 & 7, 2026, at Poornaprajna College (Autonomous), Udupi.

PRAJNA is the annual inter-collegiate fest that brings together the brightest minds and most creative talents from across the region. With a perfect blend of academic rigor and cultural extravaganza, PRAJNA offers a platform for students to showcase their skills, innovate, and celebrate.
